## Introduction

The following articles will detail our recommendations on how to write the most efficient, readable and clear code within your hx Renew models.

These recommendations are based on our in-house style, which you may wish to adopt. However, you may have your own company styles. If this is the case, we want to stress that using your defined style **consistently** is one of the most important takeaways.
